Workout Guide

4-week Beginner Routine (3 days/week)

  1. Day A — Squat 3×8, Bench Press 3×8, Row 3×8
  2. Day B — Deadlift 3×5, Overhead Press 3×8, Pull-ups (assisted) 3×6
  3. Day C — Accessory: Lunges 3×10, Dumbbell Press 3×10, Plank 3×45s

Progress by adding 2–5% load per week or perform extra reps when possible. Focus on form and recovery.

Intermediate Split (4–5 days)

  • Upper/Lower split with 4 compound lifts and 2 accessory movements per session.
  • Include 1 high-intensity interval session and 1 mobility day.

Advanced (5–6 days)

Periodized plan with heavy, volume and speed days; includes peaking and deload weeks.

Diet Templates

Maintenance Sample

~ Balanced macros to hold current weight

  • Breakfast: Oats + milk, banana, 2 boiled eggs
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken, brown rice, mixed veggies
  • Snack: Greek yogurt + nuts
  • Dinner: Fish, sweet potato, salad

Cutting Sample

~ 10–20% calorie deficit, higher protein to preserve muscle

  • Protein-focused meals, smaller carb portions, vegetables and fibrous foods.

Bulking Sample

~ 10–20% calorie surplus, enough protein and progressive overload

  • Higher carbs, energy-dense meals, snacks between meals.
